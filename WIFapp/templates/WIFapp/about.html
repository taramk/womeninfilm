The Dataset

To start with, I'm not saying all of these films are feminist films. I merely hope it make it easier to find films where women might have a significant role, either in front of or behind the camera. I suspect the criteria will change over time.

The bare minimum for a movie to be in my database is that it meets at least ONE of the following criteria:

  • two out of the 3 starring actors are women
  • the top-billed actor is a woman
  • it was directed by one or more women
  • it was written by one or more women (including writer, screenwriter, and novel roles)
I've also filtered by original language = English, to keep the dataset at a manageable size.

I've included movies from 1960 through 2022. The 1960s and 70s are top 100 by revenue per year (according to TMDB), and beyond that it's 240 per year. These movies are then filtered down, and I've removed any that don't meet the criteria mentioned above.
